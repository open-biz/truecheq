## Style Prompt
Build a sharp, trust-first fintech launch look with dark slate foundations, mint verification accents, and electric cyan action highlights. The tone should feel technical, credible, and fast, with layered UI cards, subtle grid atmosphere, and high contrast typography that reads clearly at video speed.

## Colors
- `#060B14` background base
- `#0E1A2B` panel surface
- `#15F2B3` verification accent
- `#35C7FF` action accent
- `#F6FAFF` primary text

## Typography
- `Space Grotesk` for headlines
- `IBM Plex Sans` for UI and body

## What NOT to Do
- Do not use generic blue-purple gradients.
- Do not use low-contrast gray text on dark backgrounds.
- Do not use soft rounded consumer app styling.
- Do not use playful motion; all movement should feel intentional and product-grade.
